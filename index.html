Welcome to FAMS Lab

Flexible AI-enabled Mechatronic Systems Lab is where engineering meets intelligence. Explore innovations in mechatronics and generative AI.

About Us

FAMS Lab is a multidisciplinary research team that empowers students to develop projects at the intersection of mechanical systems and AI. We focus on two domains:

  • Mechatronics: Robotics, sensors, control systems, automation
  • Generative AI: Neural networks, LLMs, ML, NLP and more
